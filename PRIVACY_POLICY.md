# Privacy Policy for QR Generator

**Last updated: January 7, 2026**

## Overview

QR Generator ("the App") is a free Android application that generates QR codes from text input. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.**

The App:
- Does not require user registration or login
- Does not collect or transmit any personal information
- Does not use analytics or tracking services
- Does not access your contacts, location, or other personal data
- Processes all data locally on your device

## Data Storage

All QR codes are generated locally on your device. The App:
- Does not store your input text on any external servers
- Does not upload generated QR codes to any cloud service
- Only saves images to your device's gallery when you explicitly choose to save

## Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| Storage (Android 9 and below) | To save generated QR code images to your gallery |
| Media Images (Android 13+) | To save generated QR code images to your gallery |

These permissions are only used when you choose to save a QR code image.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us by opening an issue on our GitHub repository:
